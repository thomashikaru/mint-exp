Informed Consent

This study examines how people comprehend written text in an unfamiliar language. Your participation is voluntary. You may withdraw at any time without penalty.

Your responses will be recorded anonymously and used only for research purposes. The data from this study may be presented in academic conferences or published in academic journals. If you have any questions, please contact the researcher at thclark@mit.edu.

AI Policy

No AI use is allowed during this study. Do not use ChatGPT, translation tools, or any other AI assistance.

We are interested in your thinking—how you naturally try to understand the texts. The whole experiment takes less than 5 minutes, and there is no advantage to using AI in this context.

Do not switch to another tab or leave this window during the experiment. Switching tabs will result in the study being automatically ended and your responses will not be recorded.
